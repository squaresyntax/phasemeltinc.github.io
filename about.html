About Phase Melt

Phase Melt Inc. started in 2012 as a audio sample company. Since then, it has evolved over the year into a one stop solution to all of your live event needs.

Our most popular services are live Projection Mapping, live visuals and augmented reality, audio and video production and editing, event curation and promotion and more!

We are also currently working on implimenting a blockchain project that will revolutionize the way we interact with one another musically. More on that to come.
